Sustainability:

Ceone Engineering Company Ltd is a company with a clear vision, a common sense of direction, and a culture of performance and delivery. Our aim is to create a successful, sustainable company for the long term. To achieve this, we need to balance economic, social, and environmental issues in our decision-making processes, ensuring sustainable working is integrated into the very core of our business.

Our sustainability strategy visually links our values to our core business strategy using three interwoven elements – living our values, excellent project delivery, and sustainable growth. Each aspect is intertwined, success in one element being dependent on achievement in the other two.
