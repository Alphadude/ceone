Services.

Who we are

We are an Engineering company established as a technical service provider, offering services to the oil and gas, power, Automobile, and Heavy Engineering and Construction industry in Africa—including wells’ workover, drilling, installation, construction, engineering and maintenance, repair and operations services, and manufacturing.

We also support oil and gas exploration with equipment, chemicals, personnel, and services. We help you maximize your infrastructure's long-term performance, even from unconventional sources.

We HSE

Our people are our asset. We are committed to safety and implement safety measures to safeguard precious lives. As a team, we set the standards and promote a culture where safety is a core value for each employee.

We only employ people with the right skill set, experience, and attitude to guide Ceone towards best practices in HSE. We continue to take significant steps forward and achieve consistent HSE performance outcomes—this is due in part to our stringent recruitment screening process but also our desire to make a difference where it counts.

Sustainability:

Ceone Engineering Company Ltd is a company with a clear vision, a common sense of direction, and a culture of performance and delivery. Our aim is to create a successful, sustainable company for the long term. To achieve this, we need to balance economic, social, and environmental issues in our decision-making processes, ensuring sustainable working is integrated into the very core of our business.

Our sustainability strategy visually links our values to our core business strategy using three interwoven elements – living our values, excellent project delivery, and sustainable growth. Each aspect is intertwined, success in one element being dependent on achievement in the other two.
